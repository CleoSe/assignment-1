About the Project

Our project is a web application that will allow S and L garage doors more easily reach out to customers and plan around them. The public pages of the site will allow customers to learn more about the company and its services, while the admin pages will allow S and L employees to see and manage customer requests and appointments received through the site. Overall, the goal of the project is to improve customer engagement whilst making the management of customers and their requests more efficient of S and L garage doors.
